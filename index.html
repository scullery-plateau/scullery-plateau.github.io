Scullery Plateau

Scullery Plateau is a suite of tools for building printable and downloadable table-top role-playing game components. The goal is to give game masters tools to help lower the barrier to entry for ttrpgs in general and in particular Dungeons & Dragons Fifth Edition.

Spritely
Pixel Art
Spritely Gallery A gallery of images created in Spritely, including datafiles.
Cobblestone
Map Builder A canvas for game boards and battle maps.
Minifier
Paper Minis Turn digital images into printable standing miniatures.
Tokenizer
Frame Images Reframe digital images into printable and downloadable tokens.
Outfitter
Character Art Create and download character art with this digital paper doll
Runyon
Dice Roller
Glyph
Icon Art Create and download icons using simple shapes
