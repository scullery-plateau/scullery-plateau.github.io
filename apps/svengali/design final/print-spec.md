# Printing Instructions

## Orientation: Page vs Card

* Printing layout will be set for a sheet of letter paper (8.5" x 11")
* Printable area of the page will be 8" x 10" centered on page
* Cards will be 2.25" x 3.5" ("Bridge Size")
* Landscape vs Portrait:
* The orientation of the paper will be opposite to that of the orientation of the cards:
  * Landscape cards on Portrait paper
    * 4 rows of 2
    * 0.25" between each row
    * 0.5" between cards on a row
  * Portrait cards on Landscape paper
    * 2 rows of 4
    * 0.5" between each row
    * 0.25" between each card on a row
